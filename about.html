About StuTalk

StuTalk, is a forum of VBIT, established in the year 2007 is the club which undertakes the official campus magazine of VBIT. Aptly described to be The Voice of VBITians, StuTalk is the forum whose roots go deep into the fields Literature and Journalism. The bi-annual campus magazine of VBIT is curated, edited and compiled by the team solely handled by students alone.

What do we do?

  • Produce college magazine
  • Hosts StuTalk's flagship event- Saadhya
  • Undertake Braodcast Journalism
  • Developed and maintains VBIT's one-stop app.
